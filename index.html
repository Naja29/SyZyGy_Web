Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in classes?

You can enroll in classes by visiting our Student Login portal, creating an account, and selecting the courses you wish to join. Our support team is also available to assist you with the enrollment process.

What are the class timings?

We offer flexible class timings including weekday evenings and weekends. You can check our Time Table section for detailed schedules for each subject and year group.

Is online learning as effective as physical classes?

Yes! Our online classes through Edumix Live provide the same quality education with interactive features, recorded sessions for revision, and direct access to lecturers. Many students find online learning even more convenient and effective.
